## Reading the grid
- **Overlap:** AI estimating, options pricing, dispatch, basic crew assignment, voice commands.
- **QuoteIQ ahead (gaps):** almost all **front-office** — self-serve quote, booking, invoicing/payments, full CRM, marketing, reviews, AI receptionist. For **franchises these are covered by Vonigo**; they bite only in the **native/independent** market.
- **CrewLogic ahead (moat):** **ops + integration depth** — Vonigo layering, voice *dispatch*, real telematics, disposal routing, crew brief, yard-signs.

## The two new differentiators (owner, 2026-06-23) — the real wedge
Both extend the ops/labor axis QuoteIQ's EmployeeHub doesn't reach, and both are **things the owner does by hand today** (strongest possible PMF signal):

1. **AI route-aware scheduling** — as jobs come in, AI slots each into the *optimal route* by **proximity + timing**, building/altering routes automatically instead of an owner hand-placing pins. Extends Job Router + Route Optimizer from "optimize what's there" to "decide where it goes." No competitor (QuoteIQ optimizes *existing* pickups; InstaSchedule just books into open slots).
2. **AI crew rotation & fairness staffing** — assign crews to routes using **partner rotation** (who's worked with whom), **requested days off**, and **weekend-off equity** (how many weekends each person has had off). This is **workforce optimization**, not a calendar. Nobody in the space does it — QuoteIQ stops at assign/track/pay.

**Why these matter strategically:** against an already-horizontal "Contractor CRM" (QuoteIQ), CrewLogic can't win on breadth or front-office features. It wins on **operational intelligence the owner currently does in their head** — scheduling + crew fairness are exactly that, and they're **defensible** (they compound with data and are genuinely hard to build well). These are the multi-crew, higher-tier (Pro/Enterprise) value; the **self-serve quote engine** is the solo/entry wedge.

## Segmentation implication
- **Solo / 1-crew independent →** the wedge is the **self-serve AI quote engine** (close the InstaQuote gap).
- **Multi-crew operator (the higher-value buyer) →** quote engine **+ AI scheduling + crew rotation/fairness** — the differentiators above. This is where CrewLogic out-runs QuoteIQ, and likely where the margin is.
