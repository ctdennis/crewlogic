Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

CrewLogicAI (“CrewLogicAI,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) provides AI-assisted estimating, dispatch, and operations software for junk-removal businesses. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and your choices. By using CrewLogicAI, you agree to this Policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information — name, email, business/franchise, role, and sign-in identifiers (e.g., Google authentication).
  • Operational data — estimates, jobs, appointments, pricing, proposals, customer records, and photos you upload.
  • Location & telematics — truck GPS and geofence data from your connected telematics provider, and job/site addresses.
  • Communications — messages and feedback you submit, and, where you opt in, the mobile phone number(s) you provide for text notifications.
  • Usage & device data — log data, device/browser type, and interactions, used to operate and improve the service.

How we use information

To provide and operate the service; generate estimates and proposals; schedule, route, and dispatch jobs; sync with the systems you connect; send you service-related communications; secure and improve the service; and comply with applicable law. We do not sell your personal information.

Third-party services

CrewLogicAI integrates with services you choose to connect, and shares only the data needed to provide each integration: Vonigo (CRM/scheduling), Motive and Linxup (telematics), Google (sign-in, Maps/geocoding), Supabase (hosting/database), Stripe (billing), and QuickBooks Online (accounting). Each provider processes data under its own terms and privacy policy.

SMS / Text messaging

Where you opt in, CrewLogicAI may send text messages (SMS) for operational purposes such as job, appointment, and dispatch notifications and account alerts.

  • Consent is opt-in. You will only receive texts after you provide a mobile number and agree to receive them. Consent to receive SMS is not a condition of any purchase.
  • Message frequency varies based on your account activity.
  • Message and data rates may apply, according to your mobile carrier plan.
  • Opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for help, or contact us at the email below.
  • We do not sell or share your text-messaging opt-in information, mobile phone numbers, or SMS consent with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing purposes. Phone numbers collected for SMS are used solely to deliver the messages you opted into.
  • Mobile carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Data retention & security

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service and to meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations, then delete or de-identify it. We use administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information; however, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your choices & rights

You may access, correct, or request deletion of your information, and you may opt out of SMS as described above. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights under applicable privacy laws. To exercise any of these, contact us using the details below.

Children

CrewLogicAI is a business tool and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
